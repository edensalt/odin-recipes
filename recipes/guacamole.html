Guacamole

Description

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and spices

Instructions

  1. Mash the avocado, lime juice and salt together.
  2. Mix in the vegetables and spices.
  3. Refrigerate for an hour and then serve.
