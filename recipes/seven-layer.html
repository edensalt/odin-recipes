Seven-Layer Taco Dip

Description

This taco dip is always a big hit at parties and family get-togethers. People gather around the platter until it's gone!

Ingredients

  • Refried beans
  • Taco seasoning
  • Sour cream
  • Cream cheese
  • Salsa
  • Tomato
  • Green bell pepper
  • Green onions
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Black olives

Instructions

  1. Blend refried beans and taco seasoning in a bowl. Spread the mixture onto a serving platter.
  2. Mix sour cream and cream cheese in a bowl, spread over the refried beans.
  3. Top with salsa. Layer tomato, bell pepper, green onion and lettuce over top.
  4. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese and black olives.
